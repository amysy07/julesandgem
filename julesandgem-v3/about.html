Who we are

At Jules + Gem Hawaii, we pride ourselves on being locally handmade.

All our products are hand-poured in small batches in Honolulu, using an eco-friendly soy wax blend and non-toxic fragrance oils, carefully crafted to capture the essence of local fruits and flowers.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2016, we launched online in 2017 and have since grown our e-commerce and wholesale business.

Born and raised in Hawai‘i, I wanted my candles to reflect my island home. Inspired by childhood memories of local flavors like guava, lilikoi, and mango, I hope my candles bring joy and evoke a sense of home for those who have left Hawai‘i.

We hope you enjoy our products just as much as we do making them.  Our goal is to help transform your space into a tropical escape, a place just for you, to relax and take time for your self.  You are worth it.
